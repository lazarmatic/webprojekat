Frequently Asked Questions

Here you can find some of the most frequently asked questions, already answered!

Renting and Buying Books

How do I rent a book?
You can rent a book through our eLibrary app by selecting the book, choosing the rental duration, and completing the process. You can pick it up in person or have it delivered.

What happens if I return a rented book late?
Late returns may incur a small fee per day. You can check the details of late fees in our app under "Rental Terms."

Can I extend my book rental period?
Yes, if the book is available, you can extend your rental period through the app before the due date.

Library Services & Online Store

Can I buy books online and pick them up at the library?
Yes! You can purchase books through our online store and select the "Pick Up at Library" option at checkout.

Is there a space in the library for reading and studying?
Yes, our library provides a dedicated area for reading and studying. You can also book a quiet study room through our app.

What payment methods are accepted for online orders?
We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and direct bank transfers for online purchases.

General Information

What is the eLibrary application?
The eLibrary app is a digital platform where you can rent, buy, and explore books. It also provides access to a physical library for reading and studying.

Do I need an account to use the eLibrary?
Yes, you need to create an account to rent or purchase books. However, you can browse the catalog without an account.

Where is the physical library located?
Our library is located at New York, King Stonburnn's 17A. You can visit us during working hours for book rentals, purchases, and study sessions.
